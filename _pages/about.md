Junteng Liu is a first-year PhD candidate at the HKUST NLP Group, supervised by Professor Junxian He. He graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024 with a Bachelor of Engineering degree.

## Research Interests

Junteng's research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning. His specific research interests include:

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning** — exploring how large language models can reason effectively and be improved through reinforcement learning
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)** — investigating and mitigating hallucination problems in multimodal models
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability** — studying the truthfulness guarantees and interpretability of large language models
